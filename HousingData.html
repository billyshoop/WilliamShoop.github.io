Pennsylvania Housing Issues

I was tasked with creating a compelling infographic to tell a visual story. Living in Central Pennsylvania for most of my life I have noticed the steady decline in the housing market for the old mining towns that used to thrive. Now many of them have become rundown and the quality of life has suffered. It got me wondering what causes this decline and what can we do to find a solution to get these towns back. My infographic examines this topic and presents some examples that many local governments in our area should follow up on.
